Things I am
making.

I build across research, startups, and open experiments. Some of this is serious institutional work. Some of it is me learning AI tools by shipping things people can actually use. Both matter to me.

What I am building now

These are my active professional projects. They are not side experiments — they are the work I show up for every day.

Power Haven
· Active — Research & Early Startup

Flexibility-as-a-service infrastructure connecting data centre batteries to grid dispatch signals.

Power Haven sits at the intersection of energy flexibility, data infrastructure, and grid equity. I am developing it as a research project at UCL while building toward a fundable startup. The current phase focuses on proving the dispatch model and establishing partnerships with system operators and data centre operators in the UK.

Not publicly linked yet. Interested collaborators or investors: reach out via LinkedIn.
