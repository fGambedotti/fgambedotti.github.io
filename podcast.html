About the show

A weekly conversation on systems, trade-offs, and the future.

2Humans is where we test ideas in public. Each episode focuses on one central question about AI, energy, economics, or political design — and follows the argument wherever it leads.

The goal is not to perform certainty. The goal is to think clearly, disagree honestly, and leave listeners with better questions than we started with.
